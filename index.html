    Contribute

    Designing

    We need talented designers to help make visually stunning and beautiful products. We need help with UI design for our website (including this one!), our app, and any upcoming projects we need help with. There are also tasks such as asset design and character design which are great for newcomers to the project.

    Developing

    Development is crucial for making our products reliable, intuitive, and easy to maintain across collaborators. Our development teams are currently working on the discord bot itself, a mobile app, our own custom API to let the bot and mobile app interface with the same database, our own website, and a specialised in-house tool for admins to edit the tree quickly and view changes visually.

    Other

    We need your help to give us feedback about how we can improve the bot, how we can make it fit your needs, or other features and products which you’d like to see. You can give us feedback on the bot here We’re also looking for marketers, managers, editors, translators and more, please join our discord group if you’d like to help out, we’re always looking out for people who are passionate about the project.
